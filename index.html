About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at UT Southwestern Medical Center working on multimodal foundational AI models for medicine and healthcare. My research spans knowledge distillation, efficient deep learning, 3D pose estimation, and computer vision. I completed my Ph.D. and M.S. at Kyung Hee University, South Korea, supported by the Korean Presidential Scholarship. I have published in ICLR, IEEE TCSVT, Elsevier IP&M, and Applied Soft Computing.
